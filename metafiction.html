Metafiction (ARGs, dark web)

Other legends can be directly participated with in a gamified manner. These have many forms, some of which are:

  • Cicada 3301 - a series of difficult cryptography puzzles rumored to be for a secret society.
  • Email chains - ever get an email from your elementary school friends warning you that if you don't pass it on, something bad will happen to you?

This category is a bit harder to define and find examples for, so I wasn't able to find much, but think of stories that are collectively developing, in which you are able to be part of it, not just someone listening to it.

There are different "subgenres" - the inner workings of the internet are inherently mysterious, so some narratives will have creative ways to break websites/employ internet osint (open source intelligence)

For example, maybe a website will look normal, but appending /secret to the url in place of its normal page would lead to a secret page...
